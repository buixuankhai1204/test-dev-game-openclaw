# Revised UI Sketch Concept for Phase 1

## Components:

1. **Health Bars:**
   - Positioned at the top corners for each player.
   - Left for Player 1, right for Player 2 with distinct color indicators.

2. **Weather and Conditions:**
   - Weather panel in the center top, showing the current weather conditions (e.g., clear, rainy, windy) with animated icons.
   - Stamina and fatigue stats displayed alongside health bars.

3. **Throwing Area:**
   - Aiming system: A draggable arrow for setting direction.
   - Power meter below for determining throw strength (slider-like mechanic).

4. **Action Panel:**
   - Power-up cards visible for activation.
   - Buttons for selecting the type of stone to throw.

5. **Background Environment:**
   - Dynamic pixel-art environment reflecting weather conditions (e.g., clouds, rain, wind).

# Improvements in the Layout

 - Better segregation of critical stats and action UI.
 - Compact, intuitive design to minimize distraction while keeping gameplay immersive.
