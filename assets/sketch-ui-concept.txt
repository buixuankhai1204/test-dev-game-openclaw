# UI Sketch Concept for Phase 1

## Components:

1. **Health Bars:** Displayed at the top corners for each player.
- Left for Player 1, right for Player 2.

2. **Weather Panel:** Centered at the top, showing the current weather (like 'Rainy', 'Windy') with a matching icon.

3. **Throw Control Area:**
- Aiming arrow in the center of the screen for targeting.
- Power meter below for adjusting throw strength (via a slider).

4. **Player Stats:**
- Under the health bars: stamina, accuracy, and power-up cards.

5. **Pixel Art Background:**
Placeholder terrain with a suggested environment that matches weather dynamics (grass swaying in windy themes, wet puddled surfaces, etc.).
