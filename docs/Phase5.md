# Phase 5: Launch and Expansion

## Launch Tasks

1. **Publishing the Game:**
   - Release the game on a chosen platform (web or app store).
   - Prepare a promotional trailer and marketing materials.

2. **Community Engagement:**
   - Gather player feedback through forums or surveys.
   - Address major bugs or feature requests post-launch.

3. **Monitoring and Analytics:**
   - Analyze player data to identify popular features and areas to improve.

## Future Plans

- Introduce new power-up cards and skills.
- Add alternative game modes like survival or ranked matches.
- Gradually enhance graphics and animations to keep the game fresh.
